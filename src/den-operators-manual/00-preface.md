# 00 — Preface: To the Keeper of Lanterns

Welcome, Operator.

This manual is written for you and for those who tend the Den beside you.
It is a guide to the systems, spirits, and structures that make up the Kitsune Den — a place where software is treated with the same care as a well‑kept workshop, and where each service hums with its own quiet personality.

The Den is not a datacenter.
It is a home.

A place where:
- dashboards are companions
- logs are journals
- ports are doorways
- tasks are rituals
- and the Operator is a steward, not a warden

This manual aims to balance two truths:

1. **These systems are real, technical, and require proper care.**
2. **They also live within a world of gentle lore and cozy identity.**

You will find:
- clear procedures
- readable architecture
- practical troubleshooting
- and small touches of Den personality woven throughout

Nothing here requires deciphering.
Everything here is meant to be understood, maintained, and enjoyed.

Whether you are Dara, Sage, Claude, or a collaborator stepping into the lantern‑lit workshop for the first time — this manual is your companion.

Light the lanterns.
Settle in.
The Den is ready.
